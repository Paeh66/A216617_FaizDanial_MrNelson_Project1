1.0 Problem

Community issues such as damaged roads, broken streetlights, illegal dumping, and public complaints are common problems faced by residents in many cities and residential areas. However, existing reporting methods are often slow, inconvenient, and not centralized. Many citizens also do not know which agency should handle their complaints, causing delays in reporting and issue management. 


2.0 Impact of the problem

When community issues are not reported or handled quickly, they can negatively affect public safety, cleanliness, infrastructure quality, and the overall wellbeing of residents. Delayed action may also reduce public trust in local services and discourage citizens from participating in community improvement activities. 


3.0 Proposed Solution

CityReport is a mobile application developed to support SDG 11: Sustainable Cities & Communities by providing a centralized platform for reporting community issues. The app allows users to submit reports, choose related agencies, track report status, and view issue statistics more easily. This solution helps improve communication between citizens and agencies while encouraging safer and more sustainable communities.
